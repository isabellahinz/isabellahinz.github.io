My name is Isabella Hinz. I have been involved in theater since 7th grade. My first ever production was Shrek the Musical and this production really made me want to continue doing theater. I have been involved in multiple shows since high school and I have enjoyed each one. I love being backstage and moving set pieces, it makes me incredibly happy. While I have always leaned towards backstage I have strong experiences with front of house and I am just starting to explore sound. I have over 500 hours in the theater with the number constantly increasing. I love theater more than anything and I hope to pursue stage managing in college.
